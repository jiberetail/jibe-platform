## Asset and rights blockers

- Client names and logos require current-relationship and public-use approval.
- Fan Town, MLB, NHL, Yankees, Jets, team, venue, catalog, and league marks or imagery require written approval. Jets material must not be used.
- Retail podium renders require approval for the environment, wrap, on-screen experience, and any third-party marks.
- Jibe Pro and Jibe AI screenshots require a field-by-field review for names, dates, figures, QR destinations, customer references, demonstration/test data, and performance claims.
- AI screenshots that display accuracy values must be approved or replaced with sanitized, owner-approved product illustrations. A disclosure alone is not evidence or rights clearance.
- Leadership portraits and all other photography require provenance and publication rights.
- Create a per-asset rights manifest before launch; the generic attribution file is not sufficient.
